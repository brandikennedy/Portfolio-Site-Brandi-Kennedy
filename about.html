About

Howdy, my name is Brandi Kennedy.

I'm a UX/UI Designer based in Austin, TX.

I'm an Alabama native who's spent the last few years calling Chicago, Nashville, Los Angeles, and currently Austin home. I started life after college by moving to a new city because of a strong desire to see more parts of the US. I became involved in the music scene in Chicago and began booking shows. Through workign with bands adn venues, I became interested in print design and visual art. I also became interested in show photography and have been dabblign in these medias ever since.

I'm currently learning about UX, UI, and Web design. I'm hoping to add these skills to my current eye for aesthetics to further my creative capabilities.
